# Olivia Sun - Gold Award

**School: Jesuit High School**  
**Category: Teens (11-15)**  
**Hours: 130**  

Olivia is a sophomore in the Portland, Oregon area passionate about youth advocacy in policy and government. She serves on the Beaverton Mayor’s Youth Advisory Board as the Civics Affairs Officer, the Cedar Mill Library Teen Library Council and works on the Oregon Health Advisory Youth Advisory Council as well as its subgroup, the Youth Data Council. Besides these positions, Olivia competes in speech and debate, serving as the Junior Director for Tumwater Middle School’s speech and debate program, and plays the flute, participating in the Western International Band Clinic and volunteering for the Valley Catholic Music School’s Summer Music Program. In her free time, you can find her trying out new restaurants or shopping. 

Oregon Chinese Coalition is proud to recognize the volunteers who consistently take the time to make a difference in the lives of others. Therefore, it is a great honor to present the Community Volunteer Service Award (CVSA) to Olivia Sun.
