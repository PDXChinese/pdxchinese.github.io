# Winston Larrow - Silver Award

**School: Hope Chinese Charter School**  
**Category: Kids (5-10)**  
**Hours: 51**  

Winston has been a dedicated member of the OCC Diabolo Team over the past year, volunteering his time and energy to promote Chinese culture through performance and practice. He loves contributing to the community, and his commitment shows through the countless hours he spends mastering the art of diabolo, rehearsing with his team, and participating in cultural demonstrations. Winston has performed in big cultural events in the city of Portland, such as the Chinese Festival at Pioneer Square in downtown and the Return of Dragon in Chinatown.
