华人文化节能够继续在先锋广场举办，其中一个主要原因是众多志愿者的奉献——他们无私地投入时间，协助搭建场地和驻点值守。他们有男女老少、父母与孩子，甚至还有来自其他社区的志愿者。

许多人询问如何加入我们成为志愿者。我们将着手推进这项工作，也希望未来能看到更多来自不同社区的志愿者参与。

One of the main reasons the Chinese Festival is able to stay at Pioneer Square is due to the dedication of volunteers who generously donate their time to help with setup and hosting tables. They are men and women, parents and children, and even volunteers from outside the community.

Many people have asked how they can volunteer with us. This is something we will work on, and we hope to see more volunteers from other communities in the future.
