木兰鼓队上周日举办了成立三周年庆祝活动。这支由才华横溢、勇敢且充满爱心的女性组成的非凡团队，将鼓乐艺术带到社区内外的每一个角落，分享她们的热情，进一步拉近华人和本地社区的联系。多年来，鼓队已成为成员们真正的家园，在这里，大家通过共同的目标与团结建立起牢固的纽带，并在互相支持中培养出坚韧的力量。

祝木兰鼓队生日快乐！

Mulan Drum Team celebrated its third anniversary last Sunday. This remarkable group of talented, courageous, and caring women has brought the art of drumming to every corner of the community, sharing both their passion and their desire to build meaningful connections. Over the years, the team has become a true home for its members, a place where strong bonds are formed and resilience flourishes through shared purpose and unity.

Happy Anniversary!
