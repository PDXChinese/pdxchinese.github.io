## Message

I like camping. At the camping ground, nature surrounds us. My favorite animals, deer and foexes, live there. My sister's favorite animals are birds, such as the woodpecker in the trees. Water and trees are important parts of our nature. We breathe in the air made by tress and breathe out carbon dioxide trees need. Young trees are growing too. In my artwork, we live with nature in harmony.
