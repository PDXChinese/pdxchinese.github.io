## Message

I drew the beach near me because it's beautiful and full of animals. Some are stuck in trash, but the hand shows we can help free them. Nature is strong, but it needs us to care. When we clean up and protect the ocean, the whole world becomes brighter and safer. Even small hands can make a big difference!
