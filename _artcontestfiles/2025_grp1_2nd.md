## Message

This artwork shows the inner beauty of nature. Even if it may not seem like it, nature holds charm in its simplicity, revealing that every small part of it is alive and full of wonder. In this piece, the flowers glow subtly from the creatures hiding inside. Every petal and swirl of magic on this painting reminds us that everything has beauty, and that surrounding ourselves with the nature around us can reveal something delightful.
