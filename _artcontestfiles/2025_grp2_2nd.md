## Message

I choose to draw this artwork because nature is all around us. No need to climb mountains or drive to a beach to expeirence nature when you can enjoy it just at your backyard. Listen to the birds sing and watch them fly to the feeder. The little things around us is what makes nature truly extraoridnary. From little bugs to huge mountains, icy cold winds to a warm summer brezze, nature is all around for us to enjoy. My artwork just shows a little bit of where you can find it.
